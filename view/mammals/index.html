White Tiger

Have you ever wanted to own your very own White Tiger? Because now you can! The White Tiger is a majestic creature of the snowfields. Taking care of this big cat is not simple, White Tigers require large amounts of meat for their energy uptake and can be very dangerous if not properly treated or provoked.



White Wolf Cub

If you are tired of the everyday canine sales in domestic pet stores, meet the White Wolf. Our White Wolves are shipped off while they are still cubs so that you can establish an easier relationship while still young. It is known that getting on friendly terms with a full grown wolf is very difficult so we provide our services this way for your best benefit.



Lemur
